About Hyperfy

Hyperfy is a Discord server founded on the 2nd of October, 2021 with the single goal of providing another community to Hypixel Skyblock, a gamemode on the Hypixel Network in Minecraft, while also offering help and carries inside the gamemode to those who need it.
